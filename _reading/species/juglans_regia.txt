#plantat website
A large deciduous tree, commonly planted in urban areas and sometimes also in the wild in secondary woodland and hedgerows, and on river banks, field-borders and roadsides. It is often self-sown or buried by grey squirrels, especially in the south and west, with plants appearing on waste ground, in copses, rough grassland, gravel-pits and on railway embankments. Lowland.
